Call for contributions

You are invited to participate in our workshop, with or without a submission. To foster community building, we encourage submitting a short paper on your work—be it current, past, or future—related to the topics listed below. The 3rd Edition of MultiTTrust will be held at HHAI25 in Pisa, Italy, on the 9th June 2025.

All submissions must adhere to CEUR-WS formatting guidelines (single-column). We will invite authors to archive these contributions at CEUR Workshop Proceedings (CEUR-WS.org) after the workshop, but there will be an opportunity to opt-out of this.

Submissions should be written in English. Please anonymize your submissions. Submit your contributions through EasyChair.


Types of contributions:


  • Extended Abstracts (3 pages): Introduce the author(s) and their interest in the topic. These can be overviews of past work, preliminary work in progress, or plans for future research. The goal of these papers is to serve as a base for introducing the participant's to each-others work and expertise. A 5-minute presentation will accompany these submissions.
  • Progress Papers (up to 7 pages): Describe a specific piece of new work, including work in progress or recently published work (published in 2023 or later). These submissions should share new results and insights, and facilitate discussions on ongoing projects. A 10-minute presentation will accompany these submissions.
